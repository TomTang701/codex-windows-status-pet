## Root-cause statement

The withdrawn bootstrap HWND's primary 120-DPI metric authority is the proven root cause. The exact legal secondary coordinate changes only when `safe_position()` passes the inflated `350x146` dimensions to `recover_position()`. The clamp output equals `secondary_right - 350` and `secondary_bottom - 146`; later target 96-DPI metrics are correct while the clamped coordinate remains.
